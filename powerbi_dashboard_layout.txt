Goal: Highlight customer pain points and rising complaints.

[Header Bar] (Navy #0F1923)
- Title: "What Customers Are Talking About"

[Row 1] (4 Keyword KPI Cards - Y: 80px, Height: 80px)

[Row 2] (Keyword Alerts Table - Y: 180px, Height: 240px, Width: 1240px)
- Visual Type: Table
- Columns: 
  * keyword_alerts[brand_name]
  * keyword_alerts[keyword]
  * keyword_alerts[pct_of_negative_reviews] (Format: Percentage or Decimal)
  * keyword_alerts[keyword_shift] (Format: Percentage or Decimal, e.g. "+5.4%")
  * keyword_alerts[alert_severity]
- Sorting: keyword_alerts[keyword_shift] (Descending)
- Conditional Formatting (Alert Severity):
  * "High" (shift > 20%): Soft Red background (#FADBD8) with Dark Red text (#78281F)
  * "Medium" (shift > 10%): Soft Amber background (#FCF3CF) with Dark Orange text (#7E5109)
  * "Low" (shift > 5%): Soft Yellow background (#FEF9E7) with Olive text (#7D6608)
- Title: "Rising Keyword Alerts (Shift > 5%)"

- Filters: Top 10 by frequency
- Title: "Top 10 Keywords by Frequency (Filtered by Brand Selection)"

[Row 4] (Packaging Complaint Line - Y: 440px, X: 640px, Height: 260px, Width: 600px)
